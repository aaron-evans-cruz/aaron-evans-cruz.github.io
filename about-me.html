About Me

Hello, my name is Aaron. Welcome to my data analyst portfolio website! I'm currently a data analyst with a passion for transforming raw data and numbers into actionable insights. With over five years of experience, I have a strong foundation in data cleaning, data manipulation and data visualization.

My love of data and numbers goes all the way back to when I was in a high chair when my mom would park me in front of the stove so I could watch the clock change from one number to the next. My schooling followed where math was my best and favorite subject.

As an adult and throughout my career, I have worked with diverse datasets while using a combination of analytical tools and programming languages like Excel, SQL and Power BI. I love delivering clear tables, reports, visualizations and dashboards to help lead to informed decision-making.

Continuous learning is a priority for me as I am always exploring new technologies to enhance my data analyst skill set. I am currently taking the Statistics with Python specialization through Coursera and the University of Michigan.

Please take a moment to explore my portfolio, where you'll find examples of my data analysis skills. If you have any questions or would like to discuss a potential fit with your company, feel free to reach out via my LinkedIn page. I'm excited about the possibility of using data to make a meaningful impact somewhere. Thank you for checking out my portfolio website!
